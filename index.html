01 — About Alfa

Who we are.

A few of the most common questions about the brand and what we do.

Q1 What is Alfa Tea Concept? +

Alfa Tea Concept is a Vancouver-based tea brand specializing in fresh fruit tea, hand-shaken milk tea, and ceremonial-grade Shizuoka matcha. Every drink is made to order from real ingredients — no powders, no syrup pumps, no shortcuts.

Q2 What makes Alfa different from other bubble tea shops? +

Three things: fresh fruit (sliced daily, never frozen pulp), pearls cooked every two hours for the right chew, and matcha that's actually ceremonial-grade — stone-ground in Shizuoka, Japan. We move slowly on purpose so every cup tastes the way it should.

Q3 Where can I follow your latest news and seasonal drops? +

Instagram is the fastest way — we post new launches, seasonal menus, and store openings at @alfateaconcept. You'll see Mango Series in spring, Strawberry through summer, and limited drops we don't always announce in advance.

02 — Order & visit

In-store experience.

How to order, customize, and what to expect on your visit.

Q1 Can I customize my drink? +

Absolutely — sugar, ice, milk type and toppings are all customizable. Sugar comes in 0% / 25% / 50% / 75% / 100%. Ice in less / regular / no ice. Oat milk is +$0.75. Pearls and other toppings are +$1.00 each.

Q2 Do you offer online ordering or delivery? +

Online ordering is coming soon. For now, all orders are in-store at the Burnaby location. Delivery via third-party apps (UberEats, DoorDash) will launch as we roll out additional locations through 2026.

Q3 Why are wait times longer during peak hours? +

Every drink is hand-shaken to order — nothing is pre-batched. During peak hours (lunch and after-school) wait times can stretch to 15–20 minutes. We don't offer expedited service to keep it fair to everyone in line. Thanks for your patience.

03 — Contact & hiring

Get in touch.

Press, partnerships, hiring inquiries — and how to reach us fastest.

Q1 How can I contact Alfa for press or partnership? +

Use the form on our Contact page or DM us on Instagram (@alfateaconcept) for the fastest reply. We aim to respond within 2 business days. For media inquiries, please mention "Press" in your subject line.

Q2 Are you hiring? How do I apply? +

Yes — we're hiring across all four locations as we open through 2026. Open positions and the application form live on our Join us page. No tea experience required; we'll teach you everything.

Q3 Do you do catering or large group orders? +

Catering is on our roadmap but not yet available at our Burnaby soft-open location. We expect to begin accepting catering orders later in 2026 once additional locations come online. For early inquiries, drop us a note via the Contact page and we'll keep you on the list.
